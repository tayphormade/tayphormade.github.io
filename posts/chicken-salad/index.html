Chicken Salad

1 min read

Ingredients

  • Chopped cooked chicken
  • 1:1 ratio mayo and yogurt
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • Chopped grapes, pineapple, apple, ect.
  • Chopped celery
  • Green onions
  • Salt and pepper
  • Celery seeds
  • Pinch of paprika
  • Squeeze of lemon juice

Directions

  1. Mix the mayo, yogurt, mustard, seasonings and lemon juice together.
  2. Mix in the chicken, grapes, celery and green onions
