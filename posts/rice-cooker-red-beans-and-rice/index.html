Rice Cooker Red Beans and Rice

1 min read

Ingredients

  • 1½ cup black beans
  • 1½ cup rice
  • 3 cups broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • Seasoning
    • Cumin
    • Garlic powder
    • Dried onion flakes
    • Pepper

Directions

  1. Dump all of the ingredients into a rice cooker.
  2. Cook until all the liquid is absorbed.
