Spiced Plum Oatmeal

1 min read

Oatmeal Ingredients
  • 1 cup oats
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup water

Cook and reduce oatmeal.

Oatmeal Toppings
  • 8 prunes
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Cinnamon stick
  • 1 star anise
  • Pinch ground cardamom
  • Pinch ground ginger
  • Serve with orange slices
