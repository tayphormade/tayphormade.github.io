Baked Banana French Toast Sticks

1 min read

Ingredients
  • 6 slices of bread, sliced into sticks
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 mashed banana
  • ½ cups milk
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • ½ tsp vanilla
Directions
  1. Mix eggs banana milk cinnamon and vanilla.
  2. Dip bread,
  3. Put on lined baking sheet.
  4. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes. Flip at 10 minutes.
